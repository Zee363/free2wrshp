What is Free2Wrshp?

Free2Wrshp is a platform that Brenden Praise started along side his wife, Mpoomy Ledwaba. It mainly focused on the two of them doing covers to some of the songs that ministered to them and carried them through difficult times. Free2Wrshp has grown impeccably and now features guest artists to come through and worship together. Moreover, Free2Wrshp has released its own EP(exciting🤩) that has received great views across all streaming platforms.

Brenden Praise

South African R&B/Soul and House singer, Composer, Producer, Pianist, Father, Husband..Brenden Praise wears all these hats so well. Widely known for his recent Platinum selling South African Music Awards Nominated Record “Joy Joy” (2018) with house Duo Black Motion. Brenden Praise has a versatile vocal style, blending soul with a pop sensibility.With the knowledge of Contemporary music and professional training, Brenden is well-versed in studio production. Aside from singing, Brenden also produces music, and writes for other artists.

Mpoomy Ledwaba

Forbes30under30, Award winning Podcaster, Digital Entrepreneur, Founder of Wisdom & Wellness, Mother, Wife. Mpoomy does it all, she is a definitely a force to be reckoned with. She recently hosted UnlimitedFest that featured guests who are all in their healing journeys respectively who shared helpful tools on how to be well holistically. Mpoomy also has a podcast, Wisdom & Wellness, that primarily focuses on healing especially in this season of her life. She is definitely in the business of healing. When she is not girl bossing, you'll find Mpoomy at speaking engagements, interviews and hosting Dove events.
